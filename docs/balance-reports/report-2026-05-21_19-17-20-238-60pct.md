## Methodology & caveats

- Each match: both players get 20 energy, deploy greedy in home zone (cycling through their comp). Sim runs at 20 ticks/sec for up to 30s or until 153 painted tiles (60%), with early-out if no paint changes for 4s (stall).
- Player A deploys in rows 0..2 facing North. Player B deploys in rows 13..15 facing South. Slight asymmetry possible from movement timer alignment and deployment order — see Mirror match section to gauge bias.
- RNG used only for movement tiebreaks. Seeded per match; reproducible.
- "Pets deployed" reflects how many pets the budget can fit; greedy placement may leave budget unspent if home zone is full.
- This is a synthetic test — real play has multi-round planning, energy regen, positioning skill, and counter-deploying. Treat results as a directional signal, not gospel.
