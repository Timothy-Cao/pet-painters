## Methodology

- **Match flow:** up to 8 rounds. Each round: draw 3 pets randomly from comp (with replacement), attempt random placement in home zone (up to 10 retries per pet). Then run 5s execution phase. Energy regens +1/sec during execution.
- **Starting energy:** 3. Cap: 10.
- **Comp types:** 13 three-of-one + 156 two-one + 286 all-different = 455 unique 3-pet comps.
- **Per comp:** 25 random opponents Г— 10 samples (5 as side A, 5 as side B). Side-B reframed to comp's perspective.
- **Win threshold:** 153 tiles (60% of board). Stall: no paint change for 10s total across match.
- **Determinism:** per-match seeded PRNG drives both pet draws and sim ticks.
