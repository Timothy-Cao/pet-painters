## Methodology

- **Comp types:** 13 three-of-one [X,X,X] + 156 two-one [X,X,Y] (ordered X,Y pairs) + 286 all-different [X,Y,Z] (alphabetical canonical) = 455 unique comps.
- **Per comp:** plays 15 random opponents (different comp index), 6 samples each (3 as side A, 3 as side B). Side-B samples reframed to comp's perspective.
- **Team WR for pet P:** average WR across all comps containing P, weighted by sample count.
- **Energy budget 20, match cap 60s**, greedy deployment, 20 ticks/s, 4 s stall-out.
- Player A in 5×5 bottom-left corner (rows 0..4, cols 0..4), player B in 5×5 top-right corner. Bias cancelled by side-swapping.
