## Methodology

- **Comp types:** 13 three-of-one [X,X,X] + 156 two-one [X,X,Y] (ordered X,Y pairs) + 286 all-different [X,Y,Z] (alphabetical canonical) = 455 unique comps.
- **Per comp:** plays 25 random opponents (different comp index), 10 samples each (5 as side A, 5 as side B). Side-B samples reframed to comp's perspective.
- **Team WR for pet P:** average WR across all comps containing P, weighted by sample count.
- **Energy budget 20, match cap 30s**, greedy deployment, 20 ticks/s, 4 s stall-out.
- Player A in rows 0..2 (North), player B in rows 13..15 (South). Bias cancelled by side-swapping.
